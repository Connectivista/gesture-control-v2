Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Camera Data

GestureControl uses your camera solely for hand gesture recognition. Your camera feed is never sent to any server — all processing happens locally in your browser using on-device machine learning.

Spotify Integration

When you connect your Spotify account, we only request permissions necessary for music playback control. Your Spotify credentials are never stored on our servers. Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser.

YouTube Integration

YouTube videos are played through YouTube's own infrastructure. YouTube's privacy policy applies to all video playback. We use the YouTube Data API to enable video search functionality.

Cookies & Local Storage

GestureControl uses browser local storage only to save user preferences such as onboarding status and authentication tokens. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

Advertising

This website may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
